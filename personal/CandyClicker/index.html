Description

This game is super simple where all you have to do is click the button to collect candy. The objective of the game is to get as much candy as you can and collect items you find along the way. The game slowly unlocks more features as you collect more candies and eat them. This took me a few weeks to make back in May 2020, and the purpose was gain a better understanding of javascript and how to properly structure a project.
