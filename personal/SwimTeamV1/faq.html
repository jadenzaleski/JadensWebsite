FAQ

Is experience necessary?

NO, we accept all swimmers ages 5-18.

Do parents need to volunteer?

Yes-a swim meet takes a lot of volunteers to run. One parent/family member needs to sign up to work 5 meets. Also food donations are needed for home meets.

Is it a long season?

No, the season begins the Tuesday after Memorial Day and ends in mid July.
