Description

I built this website back in June 2017, and it was the very first website I ever made. There is no external framework, I coded the whole site from scratch, and it took me about a month or two working on and off. The website is not very modern, and the color scheme is not very pleasing to the eyes. The layout is not very easy to navigate, and it can be hard to find what you are looking for at times. A lot of the links no longer work since I no longer have access to the forms or PDFs. However, each page is fully responsive and able to be used on all mobile devices.

Team Bio

Meadowmooor is a neighborhood swim team that is part of the Strongsville Swim League. Our team is comprised of children and grandchildren of residents from Meadowood, Co-Moor and Eastland Park. Swimmers range from ages 5-18, no experience necessary. Our mission is to have fun, cultivate community and become better swimmers. Join us today!
